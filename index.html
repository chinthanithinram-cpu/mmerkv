About Our Department

Pioneering Materials Science Education & Research

The Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at RGUKT RKV has been at the forefront of materials education and research since its establishment. Our department is committed to providing world-class education and conducting cutting-edge research in various domains of materials science and engineering.

We offer comprehensive undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs that combine fundamental principles with practical applications, preparing students for successful careers in academia, industry, and research organizations.

Research Areas

Nanoparticle Synthesis

Development of novel methods for synthesizing nanoparticles with controlled size, shape, and properties for various applications.

Carbon Nanotubes

Research on the synthesis, characterization, and application of carbon nanotubes in electronics and composite materials.

Dr. Rajesh Kumar Completed

Quantum Dots

Investigation of semiconductor quantum dots for applications in displays, sensors, and biomedical imaging.

Dr. Anil Reddy Ongoing

Advanced Steel Alloys

Lightweight Alloys
